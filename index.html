Justin Le

I am an M.S. student in the Department of Electrical Engineering at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas (UNLV), under the advisement of Prof. P. Kachroo. I received the B.S. degree in electrical engineering from UNLV in 2016, with a minor in mathematics.

My research aims to develop stochastic and geometric tools used in the design of algorithms for control, communication, and signal processing. I received the Outstanding Undergraduate Research Award at UNLV in 2016.
