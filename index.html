Bio

I am an M.S. student in the Department of Electrical Engineering at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas (UNLV), under the advisement of Prof. P. Kachroo. I received the B.S. degree in electrical engineering from UNLV in 2016, with a minor in mathematics. I received the Outstanding Undergraduate Research Award at UNLV in 2016.

In the fall of 2018, I will join the Center for Control, Dynamical Systems, and Computation (CCDC) at the University of California in Santa Barbara (UCSB) as a Ph.D. student in Electrical & Computer Engineering.
