I study nonconvex optimization problems, namely those that arise in: (1) the control of cyber-physical systems and (2) the fitting of predictive models. My research aims to develop new algorithms for solving these problems, as well as new mathematical tools for describing their effectiveness, efficiency, and robustness. I mainly work with techniques that lie at the intersection of dynamical systems, variational analysis, stochastic processes, and differential geometry/topology.
